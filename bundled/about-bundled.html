About StoryBot

What is StoryBot?

StoryBot is a collaborative storytelling Discord bot powered by Gemini AI. It enables users to co-author stories and engage with each other in a structured, gamified format.

Our mission is to bring communities together through the power of shared storytelling. While we use AI, it takes a backseat, putting the focus on human creativity and interaction. The Gemini AI will simply create helpful summaries, spark ideas, and keep narratives flowing.

Key Features

Collaborative Storytelling

Users build stories one message at a time, creating a seamless narrative flow that everyone can contribute to.

Gemini AI Assistance

Automatically generate plot twists, summaries, and more to enhance your storytelling experience.

Story Tracking

Full story history with options to save and export your collaborative creations to Google Docs.

Easy Integration

Simple setup process to add StoryBot to your Discord server and start creating stories immediately.

Available Commands

Core Story Commands

  • /startstory <your text> - Start a new story with your opening text
  • /add <your text> - Add your part to the story
  • /endstory - Finalizes the story with an optional epilogue

AI Enhancer Commands

  • /recap - Summarizes recent story progress
  • /plotwist - Gemini generates and inserts a surprise twist
